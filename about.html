Physicist turned AI Researcher

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at IIT Roorkee, where my research focuses on LLM-based agents for automated extraction of material properties and structural features from scientific literature.

My journey began with a deep love for Physics — I qualified IIT JAM (AIR 771) and completed my Masters at IIT Mandi, where I studied Many-Body Localization using Fibonacci quasiperiodic sequences. That rigorous mathematical foundation now drives my AI research.

Beyond research, I co-founded EVA Ad Ventures as CTO, deploying AI models like Real-ESRGAN for 3D anamorphic billboard production. I'm an IndiaAI PhD Fellow, awarded under the Government of India's IndiaAI Mission.
